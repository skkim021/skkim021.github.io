AngularJS & Ruby on Rails

For this project, we were gained the knowledge of building a website using AngularJS for our front-end and Ruby on Rails for our backend. This was our first team project.

This project gave us a basic insight to how the two languages commucate with each other. This gave us the opportunity to work on our paired programming skills along with collaboration ideas with a group of very different people

The idea of the project itself came into fruition because a common problem our group had. The problem was, there was no source on the web in which we could trade and sell our concert tickets. Sure, there is TicketMaster, Stubhub, craigslist etc., but those sources were either unreliable or cost a premium. The solution to the problem was Tick.get. A site where people can come to sell and trade their concert tickets with no premium and a reliable source as well.
